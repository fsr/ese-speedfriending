Speedfriending

Herzlich Willkommen zum Speedfriending. Die nächste schnelle Freundschaft steht schon für dich bereit. Um zu Beginnen, klicke einfach auf Start Pairing. Du wirst in einen Jitsi-Raum weitergeleitet, sobald eine passende Freundschaft für dich gefunden wurde. Javascript ist dafür notwendig.

Speedfriending [en]

Welcome to speedfriending. The next speedy friendship just waits in line for you. To start just click Start Pairing. You then will be redirected into a jitsi room as soon as we have found an appropriate friendship for you. It is critical to allow javascript.

In case you two do not know what to talk about, we have prepared a catalogue of questions right here.

The length of your video call is not limited. If you want to discover more than one possible friendship today, you will have to end a conversation every now and then to start again.
